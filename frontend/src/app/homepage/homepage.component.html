Welcome to Yu-Gi-Oh Deck Builder

The Yu-Gi-Oh! trading card game is a Japanese collectible card game developed and published by Konami. Our web application will utilize YGOPRODECK's Yu-Gi-Oh! API to collect information about cards to help enthusiasts build their dream decks based on official rules and banned lists. The application will give users insight into their decks and help them see the cards and market prices. Users will be able to procedurally generate a deck, see how balanced their decks are, and explore a gallery of cards
